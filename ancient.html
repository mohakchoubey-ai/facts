20 Ancient civilizations Facts

1. The Indus Valley Civilization was one of the world's earliest urban societies.
2. Ancient Egypt is famous for its pyramids and pharaohs.
3. Mesopotamia is considered the cradle of civilization and the first writing system.
4. The Sumerians invented cuneiform writing.
5. The Great Wall of China was built to protect against invasions.
6. The Mayans developed a complex calendar and mathematics system.
7. Ancient Greece contributed to democracy, philosophy, and science.
8. The Roman Empire had a sophisticated system of roads and aqueducts.
9. The Egyptians used hieroglyphics as their writing system.
10. The Harappans of India had planned cities with drainage systems.
11. Ancient civilizations domesticated animals for farming and transportation.
12. The Phoenicians were known for their alphabet and seafaring trade.
13. The Olmecs of Mesoamerica created giant stone heads.
14. Ancient civilizations developed early forms of medicine and surgery.
15. The Zhou Dynasty in China introduced the Mandate of Heaven concept.
16. Ancient Persians built the Royal Road for communication and trade.
17. The Inca civilization in South America built Machu Picchu in the Andes.
18. Ancient civilizations used coins as early currency systems.
19. The Assyrians were known for their military organization and libraries.
20. Many ancient civilizations worshipped multiple gods in polytheistic religions.
